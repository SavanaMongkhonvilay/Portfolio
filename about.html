I'm a Web Development student at JCCC

  I’m currently in my third semester of the Web Development program at Johnson County Community College, where I’ve discovered a passion for both development and design. My goal is to build a career in Front-End Web Development or User Interface Design, where I can combine creativity with technical skill to craft engaging digital experiences.

  Along the way, I’ve gained experience working with Figma and WordPress, and I’ve studied HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C++, and Python. I enjoy the challenge of problem-solving through code as much as I enjoy the process of bringing designs to life on the screen.

  Beyond the classroom, I serve as President and Co-Founder of the Developing Diversity Club at JCCC. Our mission is to provide space and resources for marginalized voices in STEM, because I believe everyone deserves the opportunity to thrive in tech. Diversity fuels creativity and innovation, and I’m passionate about contributing to a more inclusive industry.

  Outside of designing and building websites, I explore my other passions, including music, gaming, and spending time in nature. Playing guitar is one of my favorite creative outlets, and I’m also a big fan of cozy games and tabletop RPGs. When the weather is nice, you’ll often find me camping and enjoying the outdoors.
